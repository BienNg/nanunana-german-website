history_edu Our Story

More than just a language course. A pathway to your future.

NaNu NaNa was founded in 2020 in Germany with a simple but powerful goal: to help Vietnamese students turn their dream of living and working in Germany into reality.

What started as a small project has grown into a trusted learning community. Since then, we have helped over 1,000 students successfully pass the B1 Goethe Exam — opening doors to Ausbildung programs, universities, and new careers in Germany.

But for us, it has never been just about passing an exam.

check_circle It is about confidence.
check_circle It is about opportunity.
check_circle It is about building a future.

With years of experience teaching German and guiding students through the German system, we understand both the language and the journey behind it. We prepare our students not only for tests, but for job interviews, workplaces, universities, and everyday life in Germany.

Today, NaNu NaNa is expanding beyond Vietnam. We now offer German courses in English and Chinese, making our proven learning path accessible to more international students.

For students in China, we also provide full relocation support — from language training and application guidance to visa assistance and cultural preparation. From your first lesson to your first day in Germany, we walk this path with you.

At NaNu NaNa, your future in Germany starts with more than a course.
It starts with belief — and a clear plan.
