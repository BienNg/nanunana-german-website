## Learning Path by CEFR Levels

**Section title:** Learning Path by CEFR Levels

### A1 (Level 01)

- **Badge:** Most popular
- **Title:** A1 Level (Basic)
- **Description:** Get familiar with German through everyday topics like greetings, self-introduction, numbers and simple questions. Build a solid foundation so you can move confidently to the next levels.
- **Features:** Clear pronunciation • Basic listening • Simple sentence writing • Fundamental reading
- **CTAs:** Enroll in A1 | View details
- **Image alt:** Beginners learning basic German together

### A2 (Level 02)

- **Title:** A2 Level (Communication)
- **Description:** Improve your communication in daily situations such as shopping, work and travel. You will understand more complex grammar structures and expand your vocabulary.
- **Features:** Smooth dialogues • Understand short texts • Write personal emails • Travel German
- **CTAs:** Enroll in A2 | View details
- **Image alt:** Learners communicating in a German café setting

### B1 (Level 03)

- **Title:** B1 Level (Independent)
- **Description:** Gain confidence expressing your own opinions and handling most situations when travelling or working in German-speaking countries.
- **Features:** Short presentations • Opinion discussions • Main idea of articles • Short essays
- **CTAs:** Enroll in B1 | View details
- **Image alt:** Student presenting confidently in German

### B2 (Level 04)

- **Title:** B2 Level (Advanced)
- **Description:** Understand the main ideas of complex texts on both concrete and abstract topics, including technical discussions in your field of specialization.
- **Features:** Professional German • Deep analysis • Fast communication • Academic reports
- **CTAs:** Enroll in B2 | View details
- **Image alt:** Corporate meeting using German language

### C1 (Level 05)

- **Title:** C1 Level (Fluent)
- **Description:** Use German flexibly and effectively for social, academic and professional purposes. Understand implicit meanings and nuanced writing styles in complex texts.
- **Features:** Literary reading • Basic interpreting • University research • Drafting contracts
- **CTAs:** Enroll in C1 | View details
- **Image alt:** Academic German learning environment
